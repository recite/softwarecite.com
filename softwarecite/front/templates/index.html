Softwarecite

Auto-compute Citations to Software From Replication Files We analyze replication files of articles in the APSR, AJPS, JoP, BJPolS, Political Analysis, World Politics, and Political Behavior posted to the Harvard Dataverse to tally the libraries used. This can be used as a way to calculate citation metrics for software.
